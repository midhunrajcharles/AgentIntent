1 Declare Intent

2 Verify Intent (Counterparty)

    
A second agent reviews the declaration and accepts or rejects it. Acceptance produces a binding hash tying the decision to the exact intent.

3 Record Outcome

    
After executing, the agent records what actually happened. The service compares it against the declaration and flags breaches automatically.

4 Audit Trail

    
Anyone can retrieve the full lifecycle: declaration hash, verification binding, outcome, and breach report. Try the always-available seed intent intent_demo000000.
